WHAT ARKY IS

A public protocol for accountable action.

A standard is adopted when independent systems can read the same artifacts, replay the same evidence, and reach the same result.

A minimal public protocol that records who acted, with what evidence, under which policy, and what actually happened.

CORE LOOP

Evidence to decision to receipt.

Arky turns messy, real-world evidence into deterministic decisions and signed receipts that any rail can verify.

01

TIM

Cryptographic evidence: who, what, when, signed and content-addressed.

02

Notary

Witness time, order, and finality so evidence can be verified later.

03

Kernel

Deterministic decisions under declared policy and assertions.

04

Settlers

Execute verbs on banks, blockchains, or devices.

05

Receipt

Signed outcome tied to the decision, rail, and anchors.

CONFORMANCE

Vectors over vibes.

Conformance is pass/fail, not marketing. A spec is Stable only once its vectors pass on two or more independent implementations. The Rust and TypeScript reference stacks pass the published vectors and are cross-checked byte-for-byte.

VECTORS

Executable checks

Validation recomputes content IDs, verifies signatures and witnesses, and executes canonicalization, Merkle, finality, and Settler vectors.

IMPLEMENTATIONS

Two stacks

arky-core and @arky/core cover the TIM to Kernel to Settler loop across Rust and TypeScript.
